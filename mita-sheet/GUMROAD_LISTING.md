## Full Description

### The problem with every other budget spreadsheet

You overspend on Wednesday. Your spreadsheet turns red.
Now what? You manually adjust the rest of the month? Do the math yourself?
Most people just give up and call it a bad month.

### How MITA works differently

MITA treats your budget as a living system.

When you log a transaction that puts you over your daily limit, the sheet automatically:
1. Calculates exactly how much you went over
2. Finds your future discretionary days (dining out, entertainment, shopping)
3. Takes a small amount from each — never more than 50% of any single day
4. Updates your remaining days so the new numbers are always accurate

Your savings are **never touched**. Your rent is **never touched**.
MITA only redistributes money you were going to spend on flexible things.

### What's inside

**7 connected sheets:**
- **Setup** — enter your income and fixed expenses once
- **Categories** — your monthly budget split by category (auto-generated, editable)
- **Monthly** — full budget summary
- **Calendar** — 31-day daily plan, color-coded green/yellow/red in real time
- **Transactions** — log your spending (the calendar updates automatically)
- **Rebalance Log** — full audit trail of every automatic redistribution
- **Tiers** — income tier reference (hidden, powers the allocation engine)

**Automatic income tier system:**
Enter your monthly income and MITA classifies you into one of 5 tiers (Low → High)
and allocates your discretionary budget using tier-appropriate percentages.
A person earning €1,800/month shouldn't budget the same way as someone earning €6,000/month.

**Priority-based protection:**
- 🔒 SACRED: Savings, Rent, Debt — never redistributed
- 🛡 PROTECTED: Groceries, Transport, Medical — last resort
- 🔄 FLEXIBLE: Coffee, Personal Care — can give
- ↕ DISCRETIONARY: Dining, Entertainment, Shopping — gives first

### How to use it

1. Open the sheet → File → Make a copy (your own Google Sheets copy)
2. Fill in Setup sheet (takes 2 minutes)
3. Click MITA Budget → Generate My Budget
4. Every time you spend money, log it in the Transactions sheet
5. Watch your calendar update in real time

No apps to install. No subscriptions. Works in any browser.
Your data stays in your Google Drive — private, offline-capable, yours forever.

### FAQ

**Does this work with Excel?**
Currently Google Sheets only (requires Apps Script for the auto-rebalancing engine).
Excel version is in consideration if demand is high enough.

**What currency does it use?**
€ by default. You can change the currency symbol in the Setup sheet.
The math works for any currency.

**Does it connect to my bank?**
No. You log transactions manually. This keeps it private and works everywhere.
Think of the manual entry as a feature — the 30 seconds you spend logging a transaction
is the moment you stay aware of your money.

**Can I edit the categories?**
Yes. Everything in the Categories sheet can be changed.
After editing, click "Rebuild Calendar" to regenerate the daily plan.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. The spreadsheet runs entirely in your Google Drive.
Nothing is sent anywhere. No accounts required beyond your Google account.
