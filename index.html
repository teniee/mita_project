FAQ

Got questions?

Most apps show you what you've already spent — a rear-view mirror. Mita calculates a fresh daily limit every morning based on what's left in your month, so you always know exactly how much you can spend today. No manual budgets, no guilt, no surprises.

We're heads-down building. Waitlist members get first access and a direct line to the team. The more you share your referral link, the higher you move in the queue — so you'll be among the very first in.

Joining the waitlist is completely free. The app will have a free tier when it launches — early waitlist members will get extended free access as a thank-you for believing in us early.

After joining, you get a unique referral link. Every person who signs up using your link moves you 10 positions up the queue. There's no cap — refer 10 friends and you jump 100 spots. Refer 20 and you could be near the very front.

You log spending manually — this is intentional. Research shows that the act of consciously logging what you spend builds awareness. Mita is about control, not just passive tracking. Bank sync is on our roadmap for a later version.
