Privacy Policy for MITA Finance

Last Updated: January 1, 2026
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

YAKOVLEV LTD ("we," "our," or "us") operates the MITA (Money Intelligence Task Assistant) mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

IMPORTANT: Your privacy is critically important to us. As a financial management app, we handle sensitive financial information with the highest security standards and comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable data protection regulations.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, full name, phone number (optional)
  • Financial Data: Income information, expense categories, budget amounts, savings goals, transaction descriptions
  • Authentication Data: Password (encrypted), biometric authentication data (stored locally on device)
  • Payment Information: For premium subscriptions (processed securely via Apple In-App Purchase - we do not store credit card details)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, session duration, interaction patterns (anonymized)
  • Location Data: Approximate location (city-level) for expense categorization (only when permission granted)
  • Camera/Photos: Receipt images when you use OCR scanning features (stored securely, never shared)
  • Log Data: Error reports, crash logs, performance metrics (via Sentry and Firebase Crashlytics)

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In: If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your email, name, and profile picture
  • Firebase Services: Push notification tokens, analytics data (anonymized)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • Process and categorize your financial transactions
  • Generate daily budget calendars based on your income and expenses
  • Provide AI-powered financial insights and recommendations
  • Perform OCR (Optical Character Recognition) on receipt images
  • Create spending pattern analyses and behavioral insights
  • Calculate budget redistribution when overspending occurs
  • Track savings goals and progress

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app features (anonymized data only)
  • Identify and fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues
  • Conduct A/B testing for feature improvements
  • Develop new features based on user needs

2.3 Communication

  • Send transactional emails (account verification, password resets)
  • Deliver push notifications about budget alerts and spending warnings
  • Notify you about important app updates or security issues
  • Respond to your support requests and inquiries

2.4 Security & Fraud Prevention

  • Detect and prevent unauthorized access to your account
  • Monitor for suspicious activity or security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

3. How We Share Your Information

WE DO NOT SELL YOUR PERSONAL OR FINANCIAL DATA TO THIRD PARTIES. EVER.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers (Data Processors)

  • Cloud Hosting: Supabase (PostgreSQL database) - EU/US servers, GDPR compliant
  • Backend Infrastructure: Railway.app - secure cloud hosting
  • Analytics & Error Tracking: Sentry, Firebase Crashlytics - anonymized data only
  • AI Services: OpenAI (GPT-4) for financial insights - anonymized transaction data only
  • OCR Processing: Google Cloud Vision API - receipt images processed securely
  • Email Services: SendGrid for transactional emails only
  • Push Notifications: Firebase Cloud Messaging

All service providers are bound by strict data processing agreements and comply with GDPR/CCPA requirements.

3.2 Legal Obligations

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of MITA, our users, or others
  • Prevent fraud or security threats

3.3 Business Transfers

If MITA is acquired by or merged with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and in-app notification before your information is transferred.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

4.1 Encryption

  • Data in Transit: All API communication uses TLS 1.3 encryption (HTTPS only)
  • Data at Rest: Financial data encrypted in database using AES-256
  • Passwords: Hashed using BCrypt (military-grade hashing)
  • Local Storage: Sensitive data stored in iOS Keychain (encrypted hardware storage)

4.2 Access Controls

  • JWT token authentication with 120-minute expiration
  • Biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID) for app access
  • Rate limiting to prevent brute-force attacks
  • Multi-factor authentication (coming soon)

4.3 Security Monitoring

  • 24/7 automated security monitoring and alerts
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Jailbreak/root detection (app won't run on compromised devices)
  • Audit logging of all sensitive operations

5. Your Privacy Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other data protection laws, you have the following rights:

5.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. Available in-app at Settings → Privacy → Download My Data.

5.2 Right to Rectification

You can update or correct your personal information anytime in the app settings.

5.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data. Available at Settings → Privacy → Delete Account. Data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

5.4 Right to Data Portability

You can export your financial data in CSV or JSON format at Settings → Privacy → Export Data.

5.5 Right to Restrict Processing

You can request that we limit how we use your data by contacting support@mita.finance.

5.6 Right to Object

You can object to certain data processing activities (e.g., marketing communications, analytics).

5.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent for location access, camera access, or notifications anytime in iOS Settings.

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Legal Retention: Some data may be retained longer if required by law (e.g., tax records: 7 years)
  • Backups: Deleted data removed from backups within 90 days
  • Anonymized Analytics: Retained indefinitely (cannot be linked back to you)

7. Children's Privacy

MITA is not intended for children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately at privacy@mita.finance.

8. International Data Transfers
